Welcome to ITRUSST

International Transcranial Ultrasonic Stimulation Safety and Standards

We are an international consortium working together towards the safe and effective application of transcranial focused ultrasound for neuromodulation. ITRUSST aims to establish consensus on expert guides, guidelines, and standardization for this neuromodulatory technique.
